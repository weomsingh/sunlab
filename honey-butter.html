Experiment 02 — Honey Butter Roasted Almonds

Status: Active

Back to Experiments

Why this flavour

We wanted warmth without heaviness, honey that lifts rather than sticks.

Flavour intent & mood

Soft caramel, butter edge, a quiet sweetness. Mood: golden hour comfort.

Texture & crunch philosophy

Layered snap, then a smooth finish that lingers.

What was tested & refined

Butter infusion timing, honey concentration, and roast temperature balance.

Best time to enjoy

Afternoons, paired with citrus tea or cold oat milk.

Still testing. Still refining.

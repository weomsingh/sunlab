Experiment 01 — Barbeque Roasted Almonds

Status: Active

Back to Experiments

Why this flavour

We wanted a smoky profile that stayed elegant, with the sweetness arriving late rather than leading.

Flavour intent & mood

Campfire warmth with a slow, honeyed finish. Mood: night lab, low light, steady heat.

Texture & crunch philosophy

Crisp first bite, then a softer melt to let smoke settle.

What was tested & refined

Smoke-to-salt ratio, roast duration, and the exact moment to add sweetness.

Best time to enjoy

Late evenings, paired with herbal tea or a cold brew.
